# Choc Chip Shortbread Biscuits

## Info

Makes 12

## Ingredients

* 1/2 cup butter
* 1 cup sugar
* 4Tb condensed milk
* 1/2 cup chocolate bits (cadbury melts) or macaamia nuts
* 2 cups flour

## Method

1. Beat butter and sugar
2. Add flour + choc
3. Roll balls and use a flour coated for to gently flatten
4. Bake 170c ~12-15m

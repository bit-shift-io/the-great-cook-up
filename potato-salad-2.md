# Potato Salad 2

Serves: 6 

Time: 

## Ingredients

* 500g potatoes (cooked and cubed)
* 1 small red onion, finely chopped
* 1 Granny Smith apple (peeled, cored and chopped)
* 2 dill pickled cucumber (chopped)
* 150 ml sour cream
* Juice 1/2 lemon
* Salt and pepper

## Method

1. Mix chopped apple in lemon juice.
2. Add remaining ingredients.
3. Serve chilled.

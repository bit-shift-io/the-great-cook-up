# Peppermint Slice

Time: 20 min + 2 hrs wait

Serves: 16

## Base Ingredients

* 225g digestive
* 115g unsalted butter
* 30ml unsweetened cocoa powder (possibly optional?)
* 15ml white sugar

## Peppermint Filling/Icing Ingredients

* 360g icing sugar (its a lot!)
* 28g unstaled butter
* 15ml peppermint extract
* 30ml hot water

## Chocolate Topping Ingredients

* 300g milk and/or dark chocolate
* 15ml neutral oil such as vegetable or macadamic oil (could do with a little less?)

## Method

1. Using marge wipe on baking pan/tin and line with baking paper.
2. Base - Crush/blend digestives.
3. Blend in cocoa powder, sugar and butter - best to do by hand?
4. Put mixture into baking pan and press down hard to make a base. Refrigerate for ~30min (try skipping this?). 
5. Peppermint Filling - Mix icing sugar, soft butter, hot water in a bowl. Ensure a thick paste is created and spread over the base. Chill for ~30min (try skip this step?).
6. Chocolate Topping - Melt choc and oil together in microwave for 30 second burts till barely melted. Spread over peppermint filling. Chill for ~30min.

1 cup

For use chicken shnitz, wedges, fries, wraps or burgers

## Ingredients

* 1 cup mayonnaise
* 2Tb minced garlic
* pinch of salt
* 1tsp tomato paste/sauce
* 1-2Tb sherry vinegar/lemon juice etc
* 1/2tsp smoked paprika
* 1/4tsp chipotle powder
* cayenne to taste

# Mushroom Soup

serves 4

## Step 1

* 2 diced onions
* 500g mushrooms
* 1tsp salt
* 2Tb butter
* 2tsp garlic paste

## Step 2

* 500g beef strips
* 1 cup frozen veg
* 3 cups of stock

## Step 3

* 1 cup (250ml) milk or cream
* 1/2 cup sour cream


## Method
1. Brown the first ingriedients
2. Add the second lot and simmer as long as you can/want
3. Add the milk and cream at the last minute

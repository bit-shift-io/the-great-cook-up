# Creamy Sausage Pasta

Time: 10 min prep + 20 min cook 

## Ingredients

* 300g pasta
* 1Tbs oil
* 500g flavoured sausages
* 2tsp garlic paste
* 1/2 tsp dried chilli (optional)
* 250ml/1cup chicken stock
* 300ml thickend cream (try light next time)
* 60g baby spinach
* 1cup green peas
* try brocolli also
* parmesan, to serve

## Method

1. Cook pasta with any forzen vegies, saving 1/4 cup of the pasta water before draining.
2. Slice up sausages and brown in oil - they should form little balls. Add garlic, chilli.
3. Add stock and simmer for a few minutes.
4. Add cream, bring to boil. Then reduce heat to medium and simmer for ~4 min.
5. Add vegies and pasta and reserved pasta water.

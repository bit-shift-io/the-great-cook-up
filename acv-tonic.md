# Apple Cider Vinegar Tonic


## Ingredients

* mix 6:1 or 7:1 - water: apple cider vinegar to 500ml
* 1/4 tsp ginger powder
* 1/2 tsp garlic powder
* 1/8 tumeric powder
* 1/8 tsp cinamon powder
* 1/8 tsp pepper
* 1/4 tsp salt
* 1/2 tsp lemon juice


## Method

1. Mix into bottle and shake well

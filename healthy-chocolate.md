# Healthy Chocolate

Serves: 4 small
Time: 20 mins prep + 1hr freeze

## Ingredients

* 1/8 cup coconut oil
* 1/8 cup coconut butter
* 1/4 cup honey
* 1 Tbl coco
* 1 Tbl cocoa
* 1/2 Tbl milk powder
* 3 dried apricots
* 1/2 tsp vanilla essence
* pinch salt

## Method

1. Boil kettle to 90 degree and sit oil and butter in to soften. 
2. Dice apricots
3. Line bowl using butter and baking paper
4. Measure oil, butter, honey, vanilla in mixing bowl
5. Microwave in 30 second increments to soften
6. While mixing (use a fork) add coco, cocoa and milk powders
7. Add apricots on top
8. Freeze

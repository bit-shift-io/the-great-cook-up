# Bacon and Egg Pie/Quiche

## Info

Serves 8

## Ingredients

* 1/2kg bacon
* 1 large onion
* 8 eggs
* puff pastry
* milk glaze
* salt + pepper to taste

## Extras

* mixed veg
* boiled diced spinach
* cheese
* 300ml thickened cream

## Method

1. Precook bacon and onion slightly
2. Puff pastry as base and top
3. Either dice and mix, or layer like a lasagnae
4. Bake 180c 25-30m

# Bread - No Knead

## Ingredients

* 1Tb yeast
* 1Tb sugar
* 3.5 cups flour
* 400ml warm water or milk
* 1.5tsp salt
* 1Tb oil

## Method

1. 6hr first rise till tripple size
2. fold dough
3. place in pan, rise 1hr
4. butter on crust to soften
5. 210c preheated for 30-40m

## Notes

# Chewy Cookies

Serves 40 cookies

## Ingredients

* 1 cup unsalted butter, melted (1 250gram block, zap in microwave for 1 minute and let cool in fridge)
* 1.5 cup brown sugar, packed
* 1 cup granulated sugar
* 2 large egg
* 2 teaspoon vanilla extract
* 2.67 cup all-purpose flour
* 1 teaspoon baking soda
* Pinch of salt
* 2 cup chopped dark chocolate, (or any chocolate of your choice)
* Flaky sea salt for garnish, (optional)

## Method

1. In a mixer or a large bowl, mix melted butter and sugars. Add egg and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth and well combined.
To the same bowl, add flour, baking soda, and salt. Mix until just combined. Do NOT over mix. Fold in chopped chocolate.
2. Bake for 9 minutes at 180

## Notes

You gotta freeze the chocolate before putting it in the cookie mix so it doesn't make the mix watery

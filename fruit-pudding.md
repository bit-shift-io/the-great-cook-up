# Fruit Pudding

serves 6

## Ingredients

* 1/2 cup (125g) margarine or butter
* 1/2 cup sugar (125g) sugar
* 2 eggs
* 1 cup (125g) sr flour
* 500g apple/fruit
* add milk/flour as needed

## Method

1. cream butter + sugar
2. add eggs + flour
3. add fruit to bottom of dish or precook with sugar + butter to make like a pudding
1. cook 40m @ 180c

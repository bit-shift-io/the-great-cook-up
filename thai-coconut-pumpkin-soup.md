# Thai Coconut Pumpkin Soup

Serves: 5
Time: 10 min prep + 15 min cook

## Ingredients

* 2 tbsp vegetable oil
* 1 brown onion, diced
* 2 garlic cloves, minced
* 3 tbsp Thai red curry paste (Maesri recommended)
* 1.8 kg pumpkin or butternut squash, peeled, deseeded, chopped into 3cm chunks
* 2½ cups vegetable or chicken stock (salt reduced)
* 400ml full-fat coconut milk
* 1 tbsp fish sauce (or soy sauce substitute)
* Crispy Asian shallots (optional garnish)
* Sliced red cayenne pepper (optional garnish)
* Fresh coriander (optional garnish)
* Roti (optional garnish)

## Method

1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Cook onion and garlic for 2 minutes until softened.
2. Add curry paste and cook for 2 minutes to intensify flavors.
3. Add pumpkin chunks and stir to coat, cooking for 2 minutes.
4. Reserve ¼ cup coconut milk. Add remaining coconut milk, stock, and fish sauce. Simmer for 8 minutes until pumpkin softens.
5. Use a stick blender to puree until smooth.
6. Ladle into bowls, drizzle with reserved coconut milk, and top with desired garnishes.

## Notes

Original: https://www.recipetineats.com/thai-coconut-pumpkin-soup/

This recipe creates exotic tasting comfort food with very little effort. The curry paste should be sautéed thoroughly to enhance its depth. Homemade curry paste yields superior flavor, though store-bought works well here.

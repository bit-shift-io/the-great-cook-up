# Lemon Honey

~1 cups 
serves 2

## Ingredients

* 4Tb white sugar
* 1/2tsp ginger
* 1/2tsp salt
* 1Tb soy sauce
* 1/2 cup chicken stock
* 1Tb cornstarch
* 1/4 cup lemon juice
* 1Tb lemon zest
* 1tsp seasame oil

## Extras

* seasame seeds
* 1/4 cup honey (no sugar)

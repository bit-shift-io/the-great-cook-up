# Everyday Hokkien Noodles

Serves: 4
Time: 14 min prep + 6 min cook

## Ingredients

* 500g hokkien noodles (refrigerated preferred)
* 350g boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into thin pieces
* 2 tbsp canola oil
* 2 garlic cloves, finely minced
* 8 green onion stems, cut into 5cm lengths (white and green parts separated)
* 1 red capsicum, cut into thin strips
* 3 heaped cups green cabbage, cut into thin strips
* 2 tbsp light soy sauce
* 1½ tbsp dark soy sauce
* 2 tbsp oyster sauce
* 1 tbsp mirin
* 1 tbsp toasted sesame oil
* ⅛ tsp white pepper

## Method

1. Mix sauce ingredients in a bowl; reserve 1½ tbsp to marinate chicken for 10 minutes
2. Prepare noodles per packet directions, drain, and rinse briefly under water
3. Heat oil in large pan over high heat; add chicken and cook 1 minute
4. Add garlic and white parts of green onion; cook 1 minute until chicken surface seals
5. Add capsicum and cabbage; toss for 2 minutes until cabbage wilts
6. Add green onion tops, noodles, and remaining sauce; toss 1½–2 minutes until noodles brown
7. Serve immediately with optional sesame seeds and green onion garnish

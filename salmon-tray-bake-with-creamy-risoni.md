# Salmon Tray Bake with Creamy Risoni

Serves: 4
Time: 30 min cook

## Ingredients

* 1 tbsp olive oil
* 460g salmon fillets (or Tassal Salmon Family Bundle)
* 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
* 250g risoni pasta
* 3½ cups chicken stock
* 1 cup cream
* 1 zucchini, thinly sliced
* ⅔ cup frozen green peas
* ½ lemon, thinly sliced into rounds
* Salt flakes & freshly ground black pepper

## Method

1. Heat oven to 180°C (160°C fan-forced).
2. Sear salmon in a baking dish with half the oil, cooking each side for 3-4 minutes until browned. Set aside.
3. Add remaining oil, garlic, risoni, stock, and cream to the dish. Bring to a boil, then remove from heat.
4. Stir in zucchini and peas. Cover and bake 15 minutes, stirring halfway through.
5. Uncover, arrange salmon on top, add lemon slices, cover again, and bake 6-8 minutes until salmon is cooked and risoni is tender.
6. Season to taste and serve with lemon wedges and crusty bread.

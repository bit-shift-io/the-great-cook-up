# Coconut Chicken Noodle Soup

Serves: 4 

Time: 10 min prep + 15 min cook 

## Ingredients

* 2x270ml can light coconut milk
* 1/2 400g pkt Asia Udon Noodles (try the whole lot)
* 150g butternut pumpkin
* 1 red capsicum
* 1 carrot
* 1 bunch broccolini
* 1 tsp ginger paste
* 2 Tb peanut butter
* 2 Tb Thai Spice mix
* 100g (1 cup) shredded chicken (try 2 cups) - bbq chicken ideal
* 1 Tb sesame oil
* 1 1/2 cup chicken stock (try 2 cups)
* 1 can of bean sprouts
* basil leaves (to serve - optional)

## Method

1. Dice pumpkin (smaller is better), Slice capsicum, carrot, broccolini. 
2. Add all ingredients into frying pan or casserole pot.
3. Cover pot and bring to boil over high heat. Cook for 10-15 min, or till vegies are tender.

## Notes

* Serve with peanuts, fried shallots or a squeeze of lime juice.

# Pud Thai

## Info

~1 cups
serves 4

## Marinade

* lemon juice
* lime juice
* salt
  
## Ingredients

* 1/2 tsp garlic powder
* 1Tb soy sauce
* 1Tb lime juice
* 2Tb rice vinegar
* 3-4Tb brown sugar
* 2Tb fish sauce
* 1-2Tb peanut oil or 1/2Tb peanut butter
* 1tsp tamarid paste
* 1/3 cup water
* 1/2Tb chicken stock

## Method


## Notes

good with chicken and shrimp/prawns

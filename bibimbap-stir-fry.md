# Bibimbap - Korean Rice Bowl

Serves 2-4

## Marinade
* 500g thin sliced beef
* 3 Tb soy
* 2 tsp ginger paste
* 2 tsp garlic paste
* 2 tsp brown sugar
* 2 tsp warm water

## Ingredients

* 2 carrots sliced
* 1 egg fried per person
* lettuce or baby spinach
* white carrot/veg etc...
* rice/noodles


## Method

1. Slice your beef into thin pieces. Marinade
2. Cook other veg + rice/noodles
3. Consume

# Wonton Soup

Serves 3

## Base Ingredients

* 1tsp ginger powder
* 1tsp garlic powder
* 1Tb cooking wine
* 1/2Tb oyster sauce
* 2Tb fish sauce
* 1/2Tb seasame oil
* 1/2Tb soy sauce

## Ingredients

* 4 cups chicken stock
* 1-2 chicken breasts
* 10-15 wontons
* buk choy quatered lengthwise
* egg noddles
* spring onion
* bean sprouts

## Extras

* grated carrot
* spinach instead of bok choy
* mushrooms
* shrimp

## Method

1. Fry chicken and put to side
2. Add sauce ingredients and fry for 20s
3. Add stock and bring to boil
4. Add chicken, noodles, wontons
5. Add buk choy
6. Sprinkle w spring onions

## Notes

Add 1/2 cup water to dilute the stock if too salty

# Massaman Curry

Time: 20 min prep + 2 hr cook
Serves: 6

## Ingredients

* 800-1kg chuck steak
* 1 jar Ayam massaman curry paste
* 400ml coconut milk (ayam brand highly recommended)
* 1 brown onion
* 3 potatoes
* 1 sweet potato
* 1/4 pumpkin
* 200g snow peas or beans
* 1 cup rice (2 might work also?)

## Method

1. Put on rice. Dice onion, steak.
2. In a casserole pot/slow cooker - brown diced steak.
3. Add onions to brown.
4. Add remaining ingredients. Use water to clean out curry paste and coconut can and make a little juicy. Cook for 1-2hrs at 180 degrees.
6. Stir in rice and serve.

## Notes

Try adding peanuts

Original: Teri Yates

# Bacon and Egg Pie/Quiche

## Info

Serves 8

## Marinade

* 1/2 cup yogurt
* 1/2 lemon juice
* 1tsp garlic powder
* 1tsp ginger powder
* 2tsp garam masala
* 1/2tsp salt
* 1tsp tandoori spice
* 1Tb brown sugar
* 1kg chicken thigh

## Ingredients 1

* 2tsp canola oil
* 2tsp butter
* 2 medium diced onion

## Ingredients 2

* 1tsp garlic paste
* 1/2tsp tumeric powder
* 1tsp coriander powder
* 1tsp cummin powder
* 1tsp garam masala
* 1tsp tandoori

## Ingredients 3
* 1tsp chilli powder
* 2 cup tomato puree
* 1 cup milk/yogurt

## Method

1. marinade
2. 1, 2, 3
3. Add meat

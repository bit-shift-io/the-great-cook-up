# Country beef 'dump' dinner

Serves: 4
Time: 10 min prep + 8 hours cook

## Ingredients

* 1kg gravy beef, cut into chunks
* 2 tbsp gravox
* 1 tbsp plain flour
* 500g baby potatoes, halved
* 200g button mushrooms, halved
* 2 carrots, chopped
* 6 shallots, halved
* 125ml (1/2 cup) Massel Beef Style Liquid Stock
* 400g can chopped tomatoes
* 225g jar tomato chutney

## Method

1. Toss beef in gravox and plain flour in the bowl of a slow cooker. Arrange potato, mushroom, carrot and shallot over beef. Pour over stock, tomatoes and chutney. Cook on HIGH for 8 hours or until the beef is tender. Serve with steamed green vegetables, if desired.

## Notes

* You could also use chuck beef steak, cut into pieces.

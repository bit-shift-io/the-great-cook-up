# Raisin Bread (Low Rise Flour)

## Ingredients

* 2 tsp yeast
* 1/2 tsp baking powder
* 1/2 tsp bicarb
* 1 tsp some combination of: sugar, cinammon
* 1 tsp some combination of: all spice, nutmeg, mixed spice
* pinch of salt (optional)
* 1/2 tsp vanilla essence (optional)
* 2 1/2 cup flour
* 300ml water

## Method

1. The order of ingredients is the order it should be added into the bread machine bowl.

## Notes

This is designed for flours that do not rise as much, i.e. organic or low glutten (e.g. Wholegrain Milling Co Organic).
The spices (baking powder, bicarb, salt etc) can be premixed and kept in a container in the fridge.

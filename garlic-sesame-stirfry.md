# Garlic Sesame

~1/4 cup

## Ingredients

* 1/2tsp minced garlic (1 clove minced)
* 1.5Tb sesame oil
* 2 Tb soy sauce

## Extras

* 1tsp sesame seeds
* 1tsp honey or brown sugar

## Method

1. Stirfry for a minute
2. Serve on steamed or fried vegies

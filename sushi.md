# Sushi

Serves 4 (5 rolls)  
Prep 10 m   
Cook -  


## Rice

* 2 cups sushi rice
* 2 cups water
* 4 tsp sushi seasoning or rice wine vinegar


## Japanese Mayonnaise 

* 3 Tb mayonnaise
* 1 Tb rice wine vinegar
* 1 tsp soy sauce


## Ingredients 

* 2 185g tin tuna
* 1 carrot
* 1 cucumber
* 4-5 nori seaweed sheets
* surimi/seafood extender


## Method

1. Use the bamboo sushi roller covered in plasticwrap.

# Potato Salad

## Info

Serves 10

## Ingredients

* 1kg bacon
* 2kg potatoes
* 2-4 bottles potato dressing
* 1-2 capcicum
* 1-2 red onion
* 1/2 cup vinegar

## Extras

* mixed veg
* spring onions/chives

## Method

1. Peel, cut and boil potatoes till firm-soft
2. Dice bacon and onion, give a quick fry in butter and oil
3. Mix
